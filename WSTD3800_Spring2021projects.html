Class Projects:

Bates, Kiera "Police Response to Sexual Assault in the Media"
This project focuses on the character Olivia Benson, from the show Law and Order: SVU and analyzes how she,
as a fictional police officer, responds to sexually based crimes, compared to real police officers in our society.

Caley, Isabella Rose "FemFilm; Tarantino: Genius or Chauvinist"
An online film magazine analyzing violence against women in Tarantino films

Eby, Miranda "Domestic Violence During the COVID-19 Pandemic"
This is an online exhibit that analyzes four online articles that cover the issue of DV and IPV during the pandemic.
The articles are analyzed based on language and the overall potential bias based on the source the article comes from.
I used both traditionally left-leaning and right-leaning sources in this analyses.
during the COVID-19 pandemic lockdowns.

Felder, Alyssa "Law & Order: SVU Violence Against Women Project"
Analyzing Law & Order: SVU college campus sexual assaults, in relation to how the universities as well as the NYPD handle the cases.
As well as offering statistics on college sexual assault.

Jenkins, Katie "Normalizing Gender-Based Violence: Depictions of Violence Against Transgender People on Screen"
My project is an online magazine that explores the ways in which the media depicts violence against transgender people
and how these depictions serve to reinforce trans antagonism in our society

Miller, Catherine "Capitalism and Abuse in the Fashion Industry"
This website contains reflections on four eras of fashion journalism, as well as a multimedia video depicting these critiques in an exhibition form.

Morby, Lauren "Canceling The Media's Violence Against Trans Women"
My project is a website highlighting the different ways the online news sources
perpetuate the violence against transgender women.

Roever, Lillie "Sexual Assault in Visual Media"
An in-depth analysis of how the Netflix show Grand Army portrays multiple aspects of sexual assault.

Schurhamer, Rachel "Sexual Objectification of Women in Food Ads"
Showing how sexualizing women in food advertisements leads to violence against women.

Verma, Megha "Defining Domestic Violence in South Asian Households"
An analysis of media portrayal of domestic violence in South Asian families.

Villalpando, Andrea "The Evolution of Reggaeton: Portrayal of Women"
An analysis of how reggaeton has evolved over the years and how the best-known artists' songs within reggaeton render detrimental images of women, perpetuating a violence.
Simultaneously, however, I look into how women are making an appearance in the male-dominated genre and are influencing it slowly.

Yeager-Thompson, Kalie "Perpetuation of Sexual Violence in Media"
This project analyzes the Netflix series, 13 Reasons Why to see how some episodes portray sexual violence.
This project is in the form of a website that breaks up the episodes into sections with their critical analysis included.
