Digital Humanities Tools and Guides

Spring 2021

Tools:

  • WordPress
    Create blog posts and publish them, allows interaction with other bloggers
  • Scalar
    Online publishing. Create your own book with interactive features
  • Story Maps JS
    A program similar to Powerpoint and Prezzi that let's you insert images, text, and videos over a map or an image of your choosin to tell a story.
  • Omeka
    Online platform that allows you to create visually appealing exhibitions and collections.
  • Twine
    A tool used to create interactive, nonlinear stories and make games.
  • ArcGIS Story
    A program in which you can create maps and insert videos and images to tell a story. It is similar but more complex than StoryMaps.
  • YouTube
    Create your own vlog, or educational videos and upload them to your channel.
  • Steller
    Digital Social Media stories
  • Voyant
    Text data visualization through word clouds, or other graphs
  • OpenShot
    Edit video files
  • AirTable
    Data Management
  • Adobe Spark
    Program that allows you to create and design visuals, such as posters, Facebook banners etc.
